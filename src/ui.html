What does the plugin?

This plugin transforms your design into json, css variables, png's or svg's that you can use in your own code.

How to use the plugin?

You have two options to export your file.

  • Export your entire page
  • Export a selection (can exist out multiple frames)

If you choose to export a specific selection, you will have to choose how you want the design tokens to get exported. As a json file, css file, png's or svg's.

If you choose to export your entire page, you will automaticly get a json file, css-file, png's and svg's.

Next up you will get to choose what colortype you would like your files in. the options are rgb, hex, hsl or all three. After the colors you can choose wich units would like, px or rem.

After you made your choises, you can export your design tokens by clicken on the 'Export' button. If you choose to export your entire page, this can take a couple of seconds.

You will get a pop-up to download a file or a zip, download it and you can use the files in your code!
